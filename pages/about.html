Amanna Trust

The Amanna Trust (AT) is a Christian charitable organization that was founded in 2015 (Reg. No. 2094 - PIN. P051557264R) on the premise that every child deserves access to post-primary education. Since the inception of Free Primary Education in 2003, the dreams of many children have been snuffed out because of their parents/guardians’ inability to afford secondary education.
