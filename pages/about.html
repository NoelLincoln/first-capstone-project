Amanna Trust

The Amanna Trust (AT) is a Christian charitable organization that was founded in 2015 (Reg. No. 2094 - PIN. P051557264R) on the premise that every child deserves access to post-primary education. Since the inception of Free Primary Education in 2003, the dreams of many children have been snuffed out because of their parents/guardians’ inability to afford secondary education. The Trust exists to stand in the gap to enable children from poor and challenged backgrounds, e.g. orphans and needy children by enabling them access to secondary education to enhance their chances for a better future. This is achieved through the provision of financial scholarship assistance to deserving students as well as mentoring and coaching them to live productive lives. The founder of Amanna Trust was guided and driven by Matthew 25:40; "The King will reply, 'Truly I tell you, whatever you did for one of the least of these brothers and sisters of mine, you did for me.

The greatness of humanity is not in being human, but in being humane. – Mahatma Gandhi

Our Mission

  • To shape possibilities of under privileged children by providing a stepping stone that enables them to access education, thus empowering them to positively impact their communities.
